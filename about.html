Our Concept

Designed for UQ Students, by UQ High-Achievers.

SaveYourUQGrade was born out of a simple observation: university lectures are great for theory, but mastering the specific marking criteria of UQ courses requires a different approach.

Our concept is built on targeted intervention. We don't just teach content; we teach you how to navigate your specific assignments and exams. By matching you with mentors who have excelled in the exact same units, we turn academic stress into a strategic advantage.
